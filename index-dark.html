    AI-Powered Resume Analyzer (LinkedIn Integration)...

    Read More

    Traditional resume screening is a time-consuming and inefficient process, often leading to missed opportunities for both candidates and recruiters. Students, especially final-year graduates, struggle to optimize their resumes and match their skills to job descriptions effectively. Recruiters face challenges in manually filtering large volumes of applications, leading to potential biases and delays in hiring.

    This project aims to develop an AI-powered Resume Analyzer that leverages AWS SageMaker and Amazon Comprehend to automate resume screening, extract key skills, and match candidates with relevant job opportunities. By integrating with LinkedIn, the system can enhance candidate profiling, provide resume improvement suggestions, and assist students in securing better job opportunities. AI-Driven Resume Matching: Uses AWS, NLP models to analyze resumes and match them with job descriptions based on skills, experience, and relevance. .

    Automated Skill Extraction: Utilizes Amazon Comprehend to extract key skills, certifications, and qualifications, helping candidates identify gaps and improve their resumes.

    LinkedIn Integration for Real-Time Updates: Fetches candidates’ LinkedIn profiles, ensuring resumes are up to date and providing a more comprehensive assessment.

    Personalized Resume Feedback: AI suggests improvements such as adding missing skills, optimizing formatting, and restructuring content to enhance job match chances.

    Real-Time Predictive Maintenance for Industrial Equipment Using AWS..

    Read More

    To develop a real-time predictive maintenance system using AWS that monitors industrial equipment health, detects anomalies, and predicts failures before they occur. This helps reduce downtime, optimize maintenance schedules, and increase operational efficiency.

    Industries face major challenges due to unexpected machinery failures, leading to costly downtime and inefficiencies. This project uses IoT-enabled sensors to monitor machine performance (e.g., vibration, temperature, pressure) and leverages AWS cloud services to analyze real-time data, detect faults, and predict equipment failures using machine learning.

    The system will collect real-time data from IoT sensors (measuring vibration, temperature, pressure, humidity, etc.), process it using AWS services, and apply machine learning algorithms to predict potential failures. The results will be displayed on a dashboard with automated alerts for maintenance teams.

    Big data

    Big Data Analysis on Air Pollution in Tamil Nadu...

    Read More

    Air pollution is a critical environmental issue that affects public health, economic productivity, and overall quality of life. Tamil Nadu, one of India’s most industrialized and urbanized states, experiences significant air pollution due to rapid urban expansion, vehicular emissions, industrial activities, and seasonal variations. Understanding the patterns and sources of air pollution requires the analysis of vast amounts of data collected from multiple sources, including satellite imagery, sensor networks, meteorological data, and government reports.

    Big Data analytics provides a powerful approach to studying air pollution by integrating, processing, and analyzing large-scale datasets. Through advanced techniques such as machine learning, artificial intelligence, and statistical modeling, researchers can identify pollution hotspots, assess the impact of different emission sources, predict air quality trends, and develop data-driven solutions for mitigation. The use of IoT-enabled air quality monitoring systems further enhances real-time tracking and decision-making.

    This study explores how Big Data analysis can help monitor and manage air pollution in Tamil Nadu, providing insights into its causes, seasonal variations, and possible interventions. By leveraging predictive analytics and geospatial mapping, policymakers and environmental agencies can implement targeted measures to improve air quality and public health.
